About NunaCraft

NunaCraft is one of the MSMEs that was established on June 5, 2023. This MSMEs comes from the city of Serang, the founder is a sixth semester student of UIN Syarif Hidayatullah Banten, whose name is Nur Amaliah. Products sold by NunaCraft are handicraft products, namely flower bouquets. Apart from selling finished flower bouquet products, this MSMEs also serves custom orders according to customer wishes.

Store Location

The shop is located at "Kampung Taman Baru RT 17 RW 06", Serang City, Banten Province, zip code 42162.
